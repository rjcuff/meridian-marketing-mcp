# Content Calendar Planner

Use your actual performance data to decide what to publish next.
Paste this into Claude Desktop after setting up Meridian.

---

## Step 1 — What's already working

Pull from Google Analytics 4:
- Top 10 pages by sessions this month
- Top 5 pages by new users
- Pages with average session duration above 2 minutes

Pull from Google Search Console:
- All keywords in positions 4–15 with more than 100 impressions this month
- Pages with high impressions but CTR below 3%

## Step 2 — Find the gaps

Based on what you found:
- Which topics are getting traffic but have only one or two pages covering them?
- Which keywords am I almost ranking for that I haven't directly targeted?
- Which high-impression, low-CTR pages could be improved vs. replaced with new content?

## Step 3 — Recommend 5 pieces of content

For each recommendation, give me:

**Title** — specific, not vague. "How to Fix GA4 Attribution" not "GA4 Tips"
**Why** — which data point makes this worth writing
**Target keyword** — pulled from Search Console
**Format** — guide, list, comparison, case study, or FAQ
**Effort** — small (under 2 hours), medium (half day), or large (full day+)

Then rank them in the order you'd publish them over the next 4–6 weeks and explain why that order.
